Top 10 Tekken 8 Combos and How to Perform Them

Blog

Tekken 8 has taken the fighting game community by storm with its intense gameplay, stunning graphics, and intricate combat mechanics. As players dive into the latest installment of this beloved series, mastering combos becomes essential for dominating opponents. In this blog post, we’ll break down the top 10 Tekken 8 combos and provide step-by-step instructions on how to perform them. Whether you’re a seasoned veteran or a newcomer, these combos will elevate your game and leave your opponents in awe.

1. The Lightning Strike Combo

The Lightning Strike is a fast and furious combo that leaves little room for your opponent to counter. It starts with a quick jab, followed by a mid-kick and ends with a powerful uppercut.

How to Perform:

– Press: LP, RP, LK, RK

– Timing: Ensure the transition between the mid-kick and uppercut is seamless for maximum impact.

2. The Rising Dragon

This combo is perfect for knocking your opponent off their feet and setting up for an aerial assault. It combines quick punches with a devastating upward kick.

How to Perform:

– Press: LP, LK, RP, Up + RK

– Timing: Execute the upward kick just as the second punch lands.

3. The Phantom Blade

The Phantom Blade is a series of rapid strikes that confuse and overwhelm your opponent. It’s a flurry of punches and kicks that ends with a spinning backfist.

How to Perform:

– Press: LP, LP, RP, LK, RK, Back + RP

– Timing: Maintain a rapid pace to ensure all hits connect fluidly.

4. The Iron Fist

This heavy-hitting combo is all about sheer power. It involves a series of slow but punishing blows that can break through an opponent’s defenses.

How to Perform:

– Press: RP, Down + RP, LP, Forward + RK

– Timing: Each move should be deliberate, giving your opponent little chance to recover.

5. The Shadow Dance

The Shadow Dance is a mix of high and low attacks that can catch your opponent off guard. It ends with a sweeping kick that knocks them down.

How to Perform:

– Press: LK, Down + LK, Up + LK, RK

– Timing: Alternate between high and low strikes smoothly to maintain the combo.

6. The Tiger Claw

Channel your inner beast with the Tiger Claw combo. It starts with a fierce double punch and ends with a powerful claw strike.

How to Perform:

– Press: RP, RP, Forward + LP, Down + RP

– Timing: Ensure the claw strike lands just as your opponent tries to regain balance.

7. The Serpent’s Bite

This combo is a series of quick, snappy moves that can chip away at your opponent’s health rapidly. It’s perfect for keeping the pressure on.

How to Perform:

– Press: LP, LK, LP, Down + RK

– Timing: Maintain a steady rhythm to keep your opponent on the defensive.

8. The Phoenix Talon

Rise from the ashes with this aerial combo. It involves launching your opponent into the air and following up with a mid-air assault.

How to Perform:

– Press: Up + RP, RP, LK, Up + RP

– Timing: Execute the mid-air moves quickly to keep your opponent airborne.

9. The Lion’s Roar

Unleash a powerful roar with this combo that combines heavy punches and kicks. It’s all about brute strength and timing.

How to Perform:

– Press: RP, RK, Forward + RP, Down + LP

– Timing: Each hit should land with significant force to maximize damage.

10. The Dragon’s Fury

The ultimate combo, Dragon’s Fury, is a devastating series of moves that can end the match in your favor. It’s a blend of speed, power, and precision.

How to Perform:

– Press: LP, RP, LK, Forward + RP, Back + LK, Down + RK, Up + RP

– Timing: Perfect synchronization is key to executing this complex combo flawlessly.

Conclusion

Mastering these top 10 Tekken 8 combos will give you a significant edge in your matches. Practice each one diligently, paying attention to timing and execution, and watch as your skills soar to new heights. Whether you’re looking to dominate online or just have fun with friends, these combos are your ticket to becoming a Tekken 8 champion.
